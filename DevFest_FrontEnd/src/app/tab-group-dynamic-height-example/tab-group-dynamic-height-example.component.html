Funding Opportunities:

Step 1: World Council of Credit Unions

The World Council of Credit Unions (WOCCU) plays a vital role in promoting and supporting credit unions globally. Their work focuses on improving access to financial services and promoting financial inclusion. By collaborating with WOCCU, we can leverage their expertise to enhance our project.

Learn more: World Council of Credit Unions

Step 2: Philanthropic Support for Financial Inclusion

Engaging philanthropic individuals, organizations, and institutions that support 100% financial inclusion is crucial. These entities can provide seed money to help individuals launch their Asset Ownership Standard (AOS) accounts, fostering a more inclusive financial environment.

Learn more: Lever for Change | The Giving Pledge

Step 3: Adoption of Asset Ownership Standard

The adoption of an Asset Ownership Standard will ensure that 100% of individuals have access to a free market-based income-producing, dividend-paying stocks account. This step is fundamental in empowering individuals economically and ensuring long-term financial sustainability.

Learn more: National Institute of Standards and Technology | ISO - International Organization for Standardization | CEN-CENELEC

Step 4: Transfer Agents for Dividend Paying Stocks

Utilizing transfer agents for dividend-paying stocks is essential to manage ownership records and facilitate the payment of dividends. These agents play a key role in ensuring transparency and efficiency in the ownership transfer process.

Learn more: Computershare | Equiniti | Broadridge | Continental Stock Transfer and Trust Company
